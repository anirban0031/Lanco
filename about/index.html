ABOUT US

What is Lanco Group LTD?

LANCO GROUP is not just a regular business venture which is actually a promoter of good living. Its aim is not just to be a corporate powerhouse but a harbinger of better living standards by providing & satisfying the needs and requirements of human beings in this decaying planet. At present, LANCO GROUP has expanded to different business sectors in Bangladesh. LANCO GROUP is now working with full enthusiasm in the areas like: food, health, education, shelter, clothing, entertainment, environment, agro business, e-commerce and mass media., social business and so on. . We are committed to develop new projects to become a distinct and vibrant player in the economy.

LANCO GROUP believes its customers must be benefited, i.e. the company is committed to be developed as a Customer Focused Organization. So, surveying the interest of valued customers is its utmost priority, the company has developed an extremely qualified support staffs to meet the expectations and ensure the customers’ satisfaction. Employees work as a team in every sense of the word. Moreover, our share holders, employees, customers, investors always work as a “one family team” to make our vision true and successful.

We are a group of companies based in Bangladesh. We have started our journey through developing and constructing properties with rich architectural and high performance real estate. We dreamt to build a house for every middle and lower middle class family. In this line, we assumed to start property trading business-first of its kind of business in Bangladesh. We are intending to become the pathfinder in property trading business of Bangladesh. We also construct LEED certify Green Factories.

LANCO GROUP started looking for the opportunities to ensure a safe and healthier planet for the people we work for. We intend to ensure significant care to the people and surroundings through our wide range of activities. Agro, dairy and fisheries, foods & Beverage projects are taken as part of our commitment to ensure safe and reliable world to our stakeholders. LANCO GROUP always dedicated toward our commitments and strive for continuous growth.

LANCO GROUP also continued a journey in the e-commerce sector through the use of modernized techniques and high-end technology. We believe that the trusted ubiquity of online interaction is more important to continue business at modern times.

Lanco Group is a property development and real estate investment company with a forward-thinking, asset-enhancing approach that has brought significant appreciation to its portfolio of residential and commercial development sites. From its beginning as a real estate company, founded in 2008 with some vision not only to give a new shape to urban lifestyle but also ensures a safe journey till the end of life of urban dwellers.

Lanco Group on the principle that long-term excellence in the development industry is achieved through a cooperative approach, pride in our projects combined with maximum value and client satisfaction. Within a short span of time, Lanco Group has grown into a renowned and dependable organization, building elegant, aesthetically pleasing structures that grace Dhaka as finest neighborhoods.

Lanco Group LTD, is also a Grand part of MH GROUP. MHPL offer apartments, corporate suites, commercial complex and villas for every budget and life style.

Lanco Group LTD believes in quality not quantity. Lanco Group uses the highest quality building materials and finishing products to ensure durability and customer satisfaction. The success of such a construction company requires specialized labors and highly qualified professional backup and Lanco Group LTD has developed a strong team of such highly skilled, experienced professionals to work under its competent management.

What MHPL DOES?

We do real estate developments through outright purchase of land and through joint venture with land owner with a view to provide quality housing to residents – our ultimate customer.

We must recognize what our customer need tomorrow - in order to develop innovative products and intelligent solutions in partnership with them to increase their comfort.

To achieve that, we need to from the best team in industry, committed and qualified employees, who together make MHPL Success By combining economic success with environmental protection and social responsibility, we ensure sustainable development, that in our contribution to a better future for us and coming generations.

Departments at MH Properties have reputation as a reliable real estate company that gives high priority to customer desire and needs, has been created through the hard work and dedication of its work force. Instead of just stressing on good academic qualification which is compulsory for the executive level employees, MH Properties has selected its staff based on their intelligence, creativity and innovation.
