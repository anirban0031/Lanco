BOARD OF DIRECTOR

Chairman's Message

Dear Friends

I take the opportunity to thank you for visiting our website and showing an interest in our group of companies. You are our inspiration showing us your interest in our undertakings and make us determined changing the world we live.

As a dynamic group of companies, we are active in a range of undertakings, including the property trading, construction and land development, agro business, food & beverage, e-commerce and mass media. We are committed to develop new projects to become a distinct and vibrant player in the economy.

Our undertakings are- Lanco Development Limited, Lanco Fast Housing Limited, Lanco Foods & Beverage Limited, Lanco Poultry Limited, Lanco Agro, Safrina Agro, Lanco Poultry Limited, Lanco Fisheries Limited, Lanco Diary, Safari Foods & Beverage Limited, Amari Bazar and inewsbd.com.

We are a group of companies based in Bangladesh. We have started our journey through developing and constructing properties with rich architectural and high performance real estate. We dreamt to build a house for every middle and lower middle class family. In this line, we assumed to start property trading business-first of its kind of business in Bangladesh. We are intending to become the pathfinder in property trading business of Bangladesh.

We started looking for the opportunities to ensure a safe and healthier planet for the people we work for. We intend to ensure significant care to the people and surroundings through our wide range of activities. Agro, dairy and fisheries, foods & Beverage projects are taken as part of our commitment to ensure safe and reliable world to our stakeholders. We are always dedicated toward our commitments and strive for continuous growth.

We have also continued our journey in the e-commerce sector through the use of modernized techniques and high-end technology. We believe that the trusted ubiquity of online interaction is more important to continue business at modern times.

We are committed to extend our service towards our society. As part of our commitment, we are intending to train and develop the skills of unemployed young people of the country, so that they acquire knowledge and skill and contribute towards their self-development. This in turns will help their family, society and country as well.

Our continuous efforts have evolved us an exciting new brand in the market. We aspire to be a leading brand with innovative initiatives, highest quality products and performance.

We know to shape our dream; we anticipate and deliver the value to best of our judgement. We are passionate about our future and look forward to continuing our efforts.
